Our Story

Founded in 2015, Pokket Cafe has been serving the community with love and quality coffee.

What started as a small corner shop has grown into a neighborhood favorite, known for our handcrafted beverages and welcoming atmosphere. We source our beans from ethical farms and roast them in-house to ensure the freshest flavor in every cup.

Our mission is simple: to create a space where people can connect over great coffee and delicious food.
